scheduling App

This project was build on React, Node.js, and mySQL.
Professors and difference cources use Piazza, Canvas, Discord or Website. There are so many! The purpose of designing a database for TAs is to better and efficiently manage TAs’ office hours. The database contains entities that represent features of a TA’s role within a course. Our goal is for students to easily access information about the next available office hours for their enrolled courses. This goal is met by the creation of a web application that displays all students and all TA office hour sessions from their courses.

Below is the demo video. No more wonder around the web for a right OH. No more studeents missing the chnage of OH. No more TA getting tons of emails asking where they are. Triggers and procedures are not shown in the video.

This project also includes triggers and procedures in the backend. Triggers check conflicts and student's enrollment type and histroy. procedures play a vital role of our main functionality. Calling different type of procedures will give the corresponding in tables.
